Biography

  • I'm currently an associate professor in School of Information Engineering at Yangzhou University. I received my B.E. and Ph.D.degrees in School of Automation from Chongqing University, in 2009 and 2013, respectively.
  • During 2019.03-2020.03, I'm visiting SmileLab at Northeastern University, Boston, under the supervision of Prof. Yun (Raymond) Fu.
  • My research interests lie in computer vision and image processing, with particular interest in deep learning based salient object detection and binary segmentation.
