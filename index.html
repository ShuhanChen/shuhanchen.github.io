Biography

  • I'm currently a lecturer in School of Information Engineering at Yangzhou University. I received my B.E. and Ph.D.degrees in School of Automation from Chongqing University, in 2009 and 2013, respectively.
  • My research interests lie in computer vision and image processing, with particular interest in deep learning based salient object detection and semantic segmentation.
